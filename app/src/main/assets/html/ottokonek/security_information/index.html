Hi, OttoKonek Partners! To help protect security in transactions, we urge you to always be alert for any
modes of fraud and to always take security measures to protect your account from fraud activities. Let us
now look at some examples of modes of fraud so that you can always be safe!
1. “Job Seeker” Mode
“Congratulations! You have passed the recruitment selection for ABC Company The interview process
will be done in XXX City on 11 October 2018. As such, kindly proceed to transfer XXX to OttoKonek
account under ABC Company at XXXXXX for your transport fee. Kindly transfer soon to proceed
with your recruitment process.”
Have you been asked to transfer a sum of money to OttoKonek account for recruitment process
purposes? Ignore it! OttoKonek never partner with any party for our employee recruitment.
Every partnerships OttoKonek has can be viewed on our official website. Furthermore, any recruitment
and selection purposes that are done with our partners are managed in a professional way without
having to ask the candidates for additional money. As preventive measure, you have to start getting
into the habit of seeking information from the organization’s official website.
2. OTP (One Time Password) Mode
One Time Password (OTP) is OttoKonek security feature in form of verification code via Text Message
(SMS). You will get a text message with an OTP when you have just registered or logged in to
OttoKonek account. OTP codes are unique to each individual and cannot be shared with anyone else,
including OttoKonek.
If you get a text message with OTP for an activity that you haven’t just done, ignore the text message.
Never share your OTP with anyone else for any reason. Strictly say no if there is anyone who contacts
you for your OTP.
3. PIN Mode
PIN is a 6-digit combination that you use to log in to OttoKonek application and is used for transaction
verifications. Like OTP, PIN is unique to each individual and is not to be shared to anyone. Never
share your OTP with anyone else for any reason, including OttoKonek. If someone has already found
out your PIN, hurry change your PIN in Account Settings and maintain its privacy.
4. Winner Announcement Mode
“Congratulations! You have just received a prize from OttoKonek! Please transfer XXXx to XXXX
account to redeem your prize!”
Have you ever received text message or phone call like that? Don’t respond! OttoKonek never asks
for fee for competitions or quizzes. Ignore the message or call for your security, or report to officials.
Every competition or quiz will always be shown on OttoKonek’s official social media (Facebook:

information)) . We will also upload the official announcement on our social medias. OttoKonek team
will also contact the winners via Direct Message (DM) on social media or through our Customer
Service’s WhatsApp.
5. Text Message Mode
One of the most common modes of fraud is via text message (SMS). The person will send certain
information to your number, usually in form of a link. Fraud may also take place on other medias such
WhatsApp, Line, or any other messengers. To prevent you from falling victim, you must be alert for
any text messages from an unknown number that claims to be OttoKonek, and to not open the link on
the text message.
